Our Story

Chowntown was created by a dedicated team of students who connected through one shared experience. Constanly struggling inside the Mackenzie Chown Complex. After getting lost one too many times, we realized we weren't alone. The confusing layout, endless hallways, and maze like design were challenges every student talked about.

So we came together with one goal: make Mackenzie Chown easier to navigate. Chowntown is built by students, for students. A simple, helpful way to find your way around without the stress or confusion. Our mission is to turn a frustrating daily experience into something clear, convenient, and accessible for everyone.
