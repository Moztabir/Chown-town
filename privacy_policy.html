Privacy Policy

We collect only the information necessary to operate the interactive map features of this website. This may include device and browser data (IP address, browser type), map interactions (searches, dropped pins), and, only with your explicit permission, precise or approximate location data from your device to show nearby results and provide routing. We may use cookies and simple analytics to improve performance and understand how the map is used. Map tiles and routing services are provided by third-party map providers who may collect data under their own privacy policies.

We do not sell your personal information. Location and personally identifiable map data are retained only as long as needed to provide the service or to fulfil the purposes described above; aggregated or anonymized usage data may be kept longer for product improvement. You can control location sharing through your browser or device settings and can request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us at chowntown25@gmail.com. This mock policy is for a school project and not legal advice.

Effective date: November 11th, 2025.
